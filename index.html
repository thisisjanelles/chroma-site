About the Conference


We are proud to bring you CHROMA, a conference centered on creativity and design here in the heart of Vancouver. There has been a growing demand for programming and innovation with less emphasis on where these genius ideas sprouted. We hope this two-day conference where locals, and even people from other parts of the world, can come together to share their stories and ideas in promoting the importance of creativity in brightening the world’s future.

We have three goals in launching this 2-day conference. The first is to promote the various aspects people can immerse themselves in when it comes to creativity and design, whether through arts and crafts, photography, gaming, film, and more. Second, to educate and expand delegates’ learning with regards to innovative design. Last but not least, we hope to create a tight-knit community that exchanges experiences and come together from different areas of expertise to collaborate for even better creations.During the event, we plan to hold speaker series panels, workshops and fireplace chats with notable speakers with regards to their experiences in creativity and design in different companies. We also plan to have interactive booths that further encourage delegate participation and promote resources delegates may use in showcasing their creative sides.

We encourage everyone to bring their brightest and most creative ideas to this fun-filled 2-day event and showcase your talents and passions!

Get Involved

Chroma would not be possible without the organizing team and volunteers who dedicate their time and effort into making the conference a success. If you have a passion for creativity and want to get involved, we have a variety of opportunities for you. Whether it’s simply volunteering on the day of, or taking on a more involved role on the organizing team, we love getting to know new people and want to hear from you!

  • Organizing Team

Organizing Team

It's not too late to become a part of the Chroma team - if you are passionate about creativity, have the time and dedication to devote to Chroma, and feel that you have what it takes, then we want you on our team! We still have several positions open and we’d love to get to know you, so don’t hesitate to apply. If you’re not sure which position you want or if there’s no specific position that currently fits you, but you still want to be involved in the team, then please feel free to send us your resume and/or any other materials anyway. Who knows? We may even make a position just for you.
